---
title: Features
description: "Features of the Allyson Mobile App"
---

- **Stay connected effortlessly:** Get instant notifications for important updates and requests, ensuring you're always in the loop.
- **Initiate multiple tasks seamlessly:** Start new tasks anytime, anywhere, with just a few taps, and let Allyson handle them in parallel.
- **Comprehensive session management:** Easily access and manage all your ongoing and past sessions to keep track of your progress.
- **Efficient file management:** Store and access your documents directly from the app, streamlining your workflow.
- **Real-time communication with Allyson:** Chat with Allyson during sessions for immediate assistance and support.
- **Quick balance reloads:** Securely top up your account balance to continue leveraging Allyson's services without any interruptions.
